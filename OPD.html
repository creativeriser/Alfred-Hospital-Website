Out Patient Department(OPD)-

OPD is the section of the hospital where patient are provided medical consultations and other allied services.
The importance of OPD is such that it is considered one of the most valuable departments of hospital.



Our Hospital has the following services that you can take like Consultant Chambers,Examinations rooms,Pharmacy,Diagnostics etc.
OPD is a department where patient receive diagnosis or treatment but do not stay overnight.
